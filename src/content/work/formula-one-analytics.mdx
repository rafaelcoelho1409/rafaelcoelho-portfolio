## Data-driven F1 — for fans and for ML practice

Formula 1 Analytics is a side project that doubles as a sandbox for sports analytics. Four sections:

- **Overview** — F1 history, iconic circuits, season legacy
- **Insights** — statistics and pattern analysis on track and driver performance
- **Seasons** — per-season recaps with race strategies and driver context
- **AI Space** — ML on race data: race outcome prediction, pit-stop strategy modeling
